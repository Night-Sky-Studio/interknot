# Inter-Knot Privacy Policy

*Last updated: March 31, 2026*

## 1. Overview

Inter-Knot is a community-driven leaderboard platform for Zenless Zone Zero. We aim to keep things simple: we only
collect the data we actually need to make the service work, and we do not sell or share your data.

This policy explains what we collect, why we collect it, and what rights you have under applicable data protection laws,
including the GDPR.

If you have any questions, you can contact us at **[contact@interknot.space](mailto:contact@interknot.space)**.

---

## 2. What Data We Collect

The website relies on public in-game profile data, which is fetched through a third-party
service ([Enka.Network](https://enka.network)). This data is necessary for the platform to function.

When you create an account through Discord or Patreon, we store basic information such as your **email address**,
**username**, **avatar URL**, and your **user ID** from the provider if available. Authentication tokens are
**never** stored and are discarded immediately after use.

We also collect **limited technical information** to operate and improve the service. This includes your **browser’s
user agent**, **basic usage data** (such as visited routes and request parameters), and **error logs** when something
goes wrong.

---

## 3. Cookies

Cookies are used to store your **session information** after logging in. The session expires automatically after
30 days.

---

## 4. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

Your data is primarily processed within the European Union. Under the GDPR, we process your data based on the following
legal grounds:

* **Performance of a contract**: to provide the core functionality of the service (accounts, builds, leaderboards)
* **Legitimate interests**: to monitor performance, improve the service, and ensure security
* **Consent**: where applicable (for example, if optional features or third-party services are introduced in the future)

---

## 5. Third Parties

We do not sell or share your personal data.

We rely on certain third-party services where necessary, such as authentication providers and hosting infrastructure.
These providers only process data as needed to deliver their services.

---

## 6. Data Retention

We keep your data only for as long as necessary to operate the service.

Account data remains stored until you request deletion. Technical logs and error reports are kept for a limited time and
are regularly cleaned up.

---

## 7. Your Rights

Under the GDPR, you have the right to access your data, request correction, request deletion, and restrict or object to
certain types of processing.

To exercise any of these rights, email us at **[contact@interknot.space](mailto:contact@interknot.space)**, and we will
respond within a reasonable timeframe (1-2 business days, but usually faster).

---

## 8. Security

We take reasonable steps to protect your data. The site is served over HTTPS, authentication tokens are not stored, and
sensitive data may be encrypted before it reaches our servers.

---

## 9. Communication

We do not send marketing emails. We will only contact you if it is necessary for service-related reasons, such as
critical issues or important updates.

---

## 10. Changes to This Policy

This privacy policy is stored in a public [GitHub repository](https://github.com/Night-Sky-Studio/interknot), and any
changes are transparent and visible through version history.

---

## 11. Contact

If you have any questions or requests regarding your data, you can contact us at: **[contact@interknot.space](mailto:contact@interknot.space)**
